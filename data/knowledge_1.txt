[Q]: How many continents are on Earth?
[A]: Earth has seven continents: Africa, Antarctica, Asia, Europe, North America, Australia, and South America.

[Q]: What is the highest mountain?
[A]: The highest mountain is Mount Everest.

[Q]: At what temperature does water boil and freeze?
[A]: Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius and freezes at 0 degrees Celsius.

[Q]: What is the longest river in the world?
[A]: The Amazon River is the longest river in the world by some measures, while the Nile is considered the longest by others.

[Q]: How does rain fall?
[A]: Rain falls from clouds when water vapor condenses into droplets.
